About

WHO WE ARE

Mini Donut is a beloved local delicacy founded by the Tee family in Quezon, Bukidnon. Established on March 7, 2023, Mini Donut has quickly captured the hearts and taste buds of the community with its irresistibly delicious and uniquely flavored doughnuts.

What started as a small family venture has grown into a celebrated brand known for its signature soft, fluffy texture and a wide array of mouthwatering flavors. The Tee family’s dedication to quality and consistency is evident in every bite, making Mini Donut a go-to treat for locals and visitors alike.

Driven by their passion for creating delightful and affordable snacks, the Tee family continues to innovate by introducing new flavors while maintaining the classic favorites that everyone loves. Each donut is crafted with care, using high-quality ingredients and perfected recipes that showcase the dedication and pride of the family behind the brand.

GROWING THROUGH INNOVATION

Since its humble beginnings on March 7, 2023, Mini Donut has experienced remarkable growth within Quezon, Bukidnon, and beyond. What started as a small family business by the Tee family has blossomed into a local sensation, capturing the hearts of donut lovers with its delicious and unique offerings. The dedication to quality, consistency, and innovation has paved the way for an ever-growing customer base. Through word of mouth and loyal patronage, Mini Donut has established itself as a staple in the community. The Tee family’s commitment to maintaining high standards while continuously introducing new flavors has propelled the brand to greater heights, making it a beloved treat for all ages.

FRESH MINI DONUTS

One of the secrets to Mini Donut’s popularity lies in the freshness of every bite. Each donut is crafted with precision and care, using high-quality ingredients that ensure softness, fluffiness, and flavor in every piece. The donuts are prepared daily to guarantee that every customer gets a taste of their irresistible freshness. From the aroma of freshly baked dough to the satisfying texture that melts in your mouth, Mini Donut’s commitment to freshness sets it apart from the rest. Whether you’re enjoying them as a morning treat or an afternoon snack, the freshness of Mini Donut makes every bite feel like a comforting hug.

BAKED PRODUCTS

Beyond their iconic mini donuts, the Tee family has expanded their offerings to include a wide array of baked goods that satisfy every craving. From soft bread and decadent pastries to specialty desserts, each product is made with the same commitment to quality that put Mini Donut on the map. The selection of baked goods reflects the family’s passion for creating homemade delights that feel both comforting and indulgent. Carefully crafted and baked to perfection, these products showcase the skills and dedication of the bakers, offering customers more than just donuts but a complete bakery experience.

SIGNATURE DONUT

Among the wide selection of delightful donuts, one stands out as the undeniable favorite—the Chocolate Donut. This iconic treat has become synonymous with the Mini Donut brand, offering a unique combination of flavor and texture that leaves a lasting impression. Whether coated in rich chocolate, dusted with cinnamon, or topped with colorful sprinkles, the Signature Donut never fails to impress. Its perfectly balanced sweetness, light and fluffy texture, and visually appealing presentation make it a must-try for both regular customers and newcomers. The Tee family’s dedication to crafting the perfect donut has culminated in this signature creation, making it a symbol of quality and tradition.
